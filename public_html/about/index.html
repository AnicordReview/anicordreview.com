About Anicord Reviews

Welcome to Anicord Reviews! We are just silly little people from Anicord providing amazing and insightful reviews of different anime/manga/games that we have been contracted. We believe that sharing our reviews of these with other Anicord members can help grow our community and also encourage discussion about the anime/mangas/games that we love!

Our very professional team of dedicated reviewers will write 100% honest reviews on various aspects of each anime/manga/game, including the plot, art/sound design, character development, and overall thoughts of the medium. Whether you're looking for recommendations or just want to laugh at the trash special reviews, we hope you enjoy our reviews!

Thank you for visiting our site, and we hope you enjoy Anicord and contracts!
